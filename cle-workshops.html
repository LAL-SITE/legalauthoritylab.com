— Common Questions

Before you register.

Do I need to know anything about AI before attending?
No. AI Basics is the right starting point if you're new to all of this. It assumes no prior knowledge. The FL Family Law workshop assumes you've used a computer and filed a motion. That's it.
Are these approved for Florida Bar CLE credit?
CLE accreditation is in process. Credit hours will be confirmed on the registration page for each program. Ethics CLE content is built to Florida Bar standards regardless of accreditation status.
What platform do the workshops run on?
All workshops run on Zoom. You'll receive the link after registration. Sessions are recorded for registered participants — not published publicly.
Is the Ethics CLE really included with paid programs?
Yes. Every paid LAL program — AI Basics, FL Family Law AI Workshop, the cohort, and every mastermind — includes the Ethics CLE at no additional charge. It's part of the program, not an add-on.
I'm not a Florida attorney. Can I attend?
AI Basics is open to all jurisdictions. The FL Family Law workshop, cohort, and FL-specific content are built on Florida law and procedure. Out-of-state attorneys are welcome to attend for general AI workflow education, but the substantive content is Florida-specific.
Can I attend a workshop before deciding whether to buy a system?
That's exactly the right move. The FL Family Law AI Workshop is designed to show you the complete system before you purchase anything. You see it run on a real file. You understand what you're buying. That's the point.
