— Is Coaching Right for You
Coaching is a fit if you:
  • Are buying a LAL system for the first time
  • Want done-for-you installation and setup
  • Need workflow optimization or process redesign
  • Want to train your team on new tools
  • Need to build custom bots for your cases
  • Want direct access to LAL attorneys
  • Are scaling and need to systemize fast
Coaching may not fit if you:
  • Are looking for general AI consulting (we're case-specific)
  • Want generic business coaching or mentoring
  • Only need occasional technical support (use email)
  • Are buying a single low-cost system (Command Center better)
  • Prefer self-service implementation (templates available)
  • Just want to buy and forget (fine, but you miss setup)

How it works.

Free 30-minute call to discuss your firm, practice areas, case types, and what you want to accomplish. No pressure. We talk through whether coaching is the right fit.

5

Ongoing Sessions

Book sessions as needed. Strategy, custom bot building, workflow redesign, troubleshooting. Sessions billed against your retainer until depleted. Renew at the end or go pay-per-use with Command Center.
