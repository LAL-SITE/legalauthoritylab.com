— FAQ

Questions answered.

Do credits expire?▼
No. Credits are yours to keep. Use them whenever you need. Monthly subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled.
Can I cancel anytime?▼
Yes. No contract. Cancel your subscription at any time. Any remaining credits stay in your account.
What if a bot fails?▼
If a bot run fails, credits are refunded. We stand behind our bots — if you don't get a usable result, you don't pay.
Can I use this in discovery disputes?▼
Yes. Pay Per Use AI is perfect for quick discovery audits, affidavit reviews, and opposing-party motion prep. All reports are attorney-reviewed, not AI.
Is my case data secure?▼
Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never retain case data. Reports are deleted after download.
Can I add team members?▼
Firm tier includes up to 3 user accounts. Solo and Starter are single-user. Each user has their own login and usage tracking.
