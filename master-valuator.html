— What Master Valuator Does

Professional valuations. Fast turnaround. Court-ready reports.

No guessing. No inflated appraisals. Flat-fee valuations backed by professional methodology and real market data.

🏠

Real Estate CMAs

Comparable market analysis for residential property. 5–10 page court-ready PDF with comparable sales, neighborhood data, and property analysis. Use for mediation settlement modeling or ED preliminary valuations.

Starting $395
🏢

Business Valuations

Complete business appraisal using income approach, asset approach, and comparable sales. Includes P&L review, income averaging, and professional opinion of value. Court-submission ready.

Starting $1,200
💼

Estate Valuations

Comprehensive estate asset valuations for probate, tax planning, and estate settlement. Includes real property, business interests, and liquid assets. Coordinated valuation across all asset types.

Starting $1,500
📋

QDRO Calculations

Qualified Domestic Relations Order calculations and present-value analysis for retirement accounts. Covers pensions, 401(k)s, IRAs, and other retirement interests with tax impact analysis.

Starting $350
🔍

Property Inspections

On-site property inspection and photo documentation. Condition assessment, structural notes, and market positioning. Adds depth to CMA or comprehensive valuation reports.

Starting $495
⚖️

Neutral Valuations

Court-appointed neutral valuation reports. Independent professional opinion suitable for contested disputes or mediation. Available as mediator-neutral engagement.

Starting $1,800
