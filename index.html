— The Difference

18 years of case files turned into workflow architecture.

Most legal AI is built by people who have never filed a motion. They build something that sounds right — uses legal vocabulary, references statutes — and then an attorney tries to use it on a real case and it falls apart.

LAL is different because it was built by someone who has practiced family law in Florida for 18 years. Every bot comes pre-loaded with actual caselaw, actual statutes, actual procedural rules, and actual example language from real filings.

  • Built from actual FMLG case files — pleadings, FAs, discovery productions, mediation agreements, final judgments
  • Anonymized, structured, and converted into workflow logic — not templates
  • Tested inside a live firm before ever being offered to another attorney
  • Every stage feeds context forward — nothing starts cold
  • QC gates at every stage — no output advances without clearance
  • Florida Supreme Court form compliance built into every draft
  • Ethics bot included with every system — no add-on, no exceptions
  • Role-stratified lanes — the system enforces who does what
