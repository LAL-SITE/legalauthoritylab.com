— Why We're Doing This

Solo and small firm attorneys need better tools.

For years, the legal tech market served enterprise. The result: solo attorneys and small firms got left behind. They either paid enterprise prices or made do with broken software. LAL exists to change that.

We're building systems that work because we know what solo attorneys need. We've lived it. Every bot is built inside a real firm on real cases. When it works for us, we release it.
