The Four Modules

Each prompt is yours to keep — tap Copy and paste it straight into your AI.

Module 01 · 30 min

Define Your Matter Types

A written, defined list of every matter type your firm handles. Deliverable: Matter Types — [Your Firm].docx

Prompt — paste into Claude
I am a practicing attorney and I need your help identifying and defining the specific matter types my firm handles. Ask me questions one at a time to help me think through every type of case I take — primary work, secondary work, anything I have handled in the last two years. Do not move to the next question until I have fully answered the current one. When we have a complete list, read it back to me and ask if anything is missing. Then for each matter type, write a two-sentence definition: what it includes and what it does NOT include. Format the full output as a clean document I can copy into Word. Title it "Matter Types — [My Firm Name]."
Module 02 · 30 min

Map Your Case Lifecycle

Your highest-volume matter type, mapped stage by stage from intake to close. Deliverable: Case Lifecycle — [Matter Type].docx

Prompt — paste into Claude
I need to document the case lifecycle for one matter type at my firm — the standard sequence from intake to close. Start by asking me which matter type. Then walk me through each stage one at a time using this three-phase structure: Phase 1 (Open), Phase 2 (Develop), Phase 3 (Resolve). For each phase, ask what specifically happens at my firm — who does what, what documents are created, what decisions are made, what can go wrong. Ask follow-up questions until every step is clear. Then produce a written lifecycle document with: phase name, stage name, who handles it, what happens, what gets created, what must happen before the next stage begins.
Module 03 · 30 min

Build Your Intake Form Structure

A complete intake form field list for your most common matter type. Deliverable: Intake Form Structure — [Matter Type].docx

Prompt — paste into Claude
I need to build an intake form for my most common matter type. An intake form is the questionnaire I give new clients at the start of representation. Start by asking me which matter type. Then ask me about my current intake process — what I already collect, how I collect it, and what I always wish I had asked earlier in a case. Then walk me through every category of information I might need, one at a time: Client identifying information · Opposing party information · Case facts specific to this matter type · Prior legal history · Documents I need from them at the start · Anything specific to this matter type I always need to know upfront. After I answer, give me a complete form field list organized by category. For each field: field label, field type (short text / long text / date / yes-no / dropdown), required or optional.
Module 04 · 30 min · Live Demo

Configure Your Claude Project

Write your project instructions and load them into a configured project, so every future session starts with your firm already in context. Deliverable: AI Project Instructions.docx + a live project

Prompt — paste into Claude
I need to write project instructions for my AI workspace. These are the standing instructions that load into every session so AI already knows my firm and does not start from scratch each time. Ask me for the following, one item at a time: 1. My name, firm name, state, and bar number 2. My practice areas and matter types 3. How my firm works and who we serve 4. What I want AI to help me with 5. What AI should never do. Then write complete project instructions in first person as if I am giving standing orders to an assistant. Direct and specific — not generic. After drafting, tell me exactly where to paste these in Claude.
