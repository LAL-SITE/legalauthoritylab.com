About Legal Authority Lab
What is Legal Authority Lab?
▼
LAL is an AI systems company built for family law attorneys. We build bots that handle discovery, drafting, GAL investigations, contempt analysis, and research. All systems are built on real legal workflows proven at Family Matters Law Group (FMLG), our one-attorney family law firm. We don't outsource. We don't give you generic AI. We give you systems tested on real clients.
Who are the founders?
▼
Three equal co-founders: Leisa Wintz (18+ years FL family law, founder of FMLG), Nazarena Hauser (Operations & Partnership), and Cheryl-Dene Spring, Esq. (CFO, 15+ years attorney/real estate, founder of Sobi Law Group, Super Lawyers Rising Star 2017–2020). We're all practicing attorneys who've built and scaled law firms.
Is LAL independent from Family Matters Law Group?
▼
Yes. LAL is a separate, independent company. FMLG is our proof of concept — we test all systems there first — but LAL is its own entity with its own revenue, clients, and roadmap. FMLG is still fully operational as a family law firm.
When was LAL founded?
▼
April 28, 2026. We launched publicly with 120 attorney sign-ups within 24 hours of our first post. 67 attorneys are currently on the waitlist. Revenue to date: $5K. Pipeline: $40K+.
Products & Systems
What systems does LAL offer?
▼
Currently live: Discovery Suite ($5,000) — 8 bots for financial disclosure review, cross-checking, and gap analysis. GAL Suite ($2,500) — investigation, reporting, chronology. Drafting Bot ($1,500) — pleadings, motions, proposed orders. Contempt Suite ($697) — contempt analysis and defense. In development: Divorce Bot, Paternity Bot, Modification Bot, Name Change Bot, Research Suite, Mediation Prep Bot (all $197). Master Valuator (coming Sep–Oct 2026) and Pay Per Use AI (coming Q3 2026) are in active development.
What do I get when I buy a system?
▼
One-time purchase. You own the bot(s) forever. Includes bot install, documentation, state ethics bot for your jurisdiction (included), and email support. Coaching available separately if you want done-for-you setup and training.
Are there subscription fees?
▼
No. LAL systems are one-time purchases. You pay once and own them. No monthly fees. No AI subscriptions required. (Master Valuator and Pay Per Use AI will have optional subscription tiers when they launch, but these are separate from the core LAL systems.)
Can I combine systems? Is there a discount?
▼
Yes. Bundle discounts apply at purchase: 2 systems = 10% off | 3 systems = 15% off | 4+ systems = 20% off. If you also buy coaching, discounts are larger: 1 system + coaching = 10% off both | 2 systems + coaching = 20% off both | 3+ systems + coaching = 25-30% off.
Do I need Claude Pro or ChatGPT to use LAL systems?
▼
No. LAL bots are built to work with Claude Pro or higher (Teams/Enterprise). You don't need a separate AI subscription — Claude Pro is ~$20/month. Some bots work with other AI platforms, but Claude is primary.

Coaching
What is LAL Coaching?
▼
Done-for-you LAL system implementation, team training, and custom bot building. A $5,000 retainer buys you a credit balance. Sessions are billed against the balance: 2-hour session = $600 | 1-hour = $350 | 30-min = $150. Your coach is Leisa or Naz, both practicing attorneys with law firm and AI experience.
Do I need coaching if I buy a system?
▼
No. Coaching is optional. You can self-install with documentation and templates. But if you want done-for-you setup, team training, or custom bot building, coaching is the way to go.
What's included in the $5,000 retainer?
▼
Your $5,000 retainer includes: (1) free 30-min strategy call (doesn't count against retainer), (2) done-for-you install of all systems you own, (3) state ethics bot included, (4) $5,000 credit balance for sessions. Sessions are booked on top of the retainer and charged against your balance.
Can I book a strategy call before committing?
▼
Yes. Free 30-minute exploration call. No sales pitch. We'll walk through your firm, discuss your needs, and explain what coaching looks like for you. Book here.
Support & Implementation
What support do I get if I buy a system?
▼
Email support included. We respond within 24 business hours. For hands-on implementation or training, coaching is the add-on service.
Can I install the systems myself?
▼
Yes. All systems come with setup documentation and templates. If you're comfortable with AI tools, you can self-install. Many attorneys do. If you want hands-on guidance, that's what coaching is for.
How long does implementation take?
▼
Self-install: 2–4 hours per system for most attorneys. Coaching-assisted: We do the install and training in your first session (usually 2 hours), then you're ready to go. Ongoing custom builds and optimizations are billed separately.
Do you train my whole team?
▼
Yes, if you have coaching. Training sessions can be included in your retainer. We run live team sessions walking paralegals and staff through the workflows, document management, and client communication protocols.
Data & Security
Is my case data secure?
▼
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Your case files are processed through your own AI account (Claude Pro or higher), not through LAL. We don't store case data. Reports are generated and saved to your device only.
Does LAL see my client data?
▼
No. Systems run locally in your Claude account. The bots process your data, but LAL doesn't access, store, or retain it. Coaching sessions are confidential attorney-client conversations.
Can I use LAL in discovery disputes?
▼
Yes. LAL reports are attorney-reviewed analysis, not AI-generated documents. The systems help you work faster and smarter, but your attorney judgment is the final gate. Perfectly appropriate for mediation, discovery disputes, and litigation.
Is LAL compliant with ethics rules?
▼
Yes. Every LAL customer gets their state's ethics compliance bot included. Florida attorneys get FL bar-specific ethics rules. Coaching includes ethics training. All workflows are designed with attorney oversight and professional responsibility in mind.
