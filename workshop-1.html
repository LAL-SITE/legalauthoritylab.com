— Workshop Overview

The Foundation Workshop

Before you build AI into your practice, you need to know what you're actually building with. This workshop answers the questions every attorney asks before adopting AI: What can it really do? What can't it do? What are the real risks? What's the actual workflow?

You'll walk through a live example of how AI handles a real family law discovery project — start to finish. See how it integrates into your existing systems. Understand where it speeds you up and where it doesn't. Leave with a clear picture of whether and how AI fits your firm.

Best for:

  • Solo attorneys who haven't used AI yet
  • Practitioners considering LAL systems
  • Firm managers evaluating AI adoption
  • Anyone who wants to understand what AI can actually do in a law practice
— What You'll Learn

The Real Picture

  • What AI can do in family law (and what it can't)
  • How AI integrates into your existing case workflow
  • The difference between general AI and practice-built systems
  • What the ethical and malpractice landscape actually looks like
  • Whether AI is right for your firm — and how to decide
  • How to start without risk or complexity
